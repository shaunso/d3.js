Econet Wireless (Pvt) Ltd

Quarter on quarter subscriber growth between Q3, 2014 and Q3, 2023

Summary

  • Econet experienced a CAGR of 5.3% in active mobile subscriber growth over the period
  • Econet has a monoply on the Zimbabwean market, with a market share of 69.8% of the 14.8 million subscribers
  • Econet's increase in market share is attributed to the overall growth of the market, in combination with Telecel subscribers switching network providers
